Why We Love Cats

In 2015, Emmett Murphy found an abandoned litter of stray kittens in his back garden. Despite being a dog person all his life, he took them in. When he adopted the kittens out a few weeks later, he found himself keeping one cat, a rascal named Kaz. It was the best decision of his life, and in 2017 he created Pawsome Pudding.

At Pawsome Pudding, we think cats are the best. Whether a cat likes to nap all day or has a constant case of the zoomies, there is something to love in each and every cat. Cats are just purrfect, don't you agree?
